Delicious Dessert Recipes

1. Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla; mix well.
  4. Pour the batter into a greased pan and bake for 30–35 minutes.
  5. Let it cool and serve with chocolate frosting.

2. Fruit Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 apple (chopped)
  • 1 banana (sliced)
  • 1 orange (peeled and sectioned)
  • 1 cup grapes
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp lemon juice

Steps:

  1. Place all chopped fruits in a large bowl.
  2. Drizzle honey and lemon juice over the fruits.
  3. Mix gently to combine all flavors.
  4. Refrigerate for 30 minutes before serving.
